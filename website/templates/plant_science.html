Plant Science Page

Welcome to our plant science page! Here you will learn all about the fascinating world of plants and how they grow.

What is a vertical farm?

Vertical farming is a system of growing crops in vertically stacked layers or trays in a controlled environment, using hydroponics, aeroponics, and LED lighting to increase yield and efficiency, and reduce dependence on traditional soil-based farming.

What are plants?

Plants are multicellular organisms that belong to the kingdom Plantae. They are characterized by their ability to produce their own food through photosynthesis, which involves converting sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide into sugar and oxygen.

Plant growth and development

Plants go through a series of stages as they grow and develop, from seed to maturity. These stages are characterized by changes in the plant's size, shape, and function, and are regulated by a variety of internal and external factors.

Plant diversity

Plants come in many different shapes and sizes, and can be found in virtually every environment on Earth. From towering trees to tiny mosses, plants have evolved to fill a wide variety of ecological niches.
