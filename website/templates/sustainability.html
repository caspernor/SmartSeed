Sustainability

SmartSeed is committed to sustainable and environmentally friendly practices, both in our vertical farms and in our operations as a company.

{% endblock %} {% block body %}
Reducing Food Waste

We use precise growing techniques to ensure that our crops are grown efficiently, reducing food waste and making the most of our resources.

Sustainable Energy

Our vertical farms are powered by renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, reducing our carbon footprint.

Reducing Water Usage

Our vertical farms use significantly less water than traditional farming methods, reducing our water usage and preserving this precious resource.

Our Sustainability Goals

  • Reduce our carbon footprint by 50% by 2030
  • Use 100% renewable energy to power our vertical farms by 2025
  • Reduce our water usage by 75% by 2025
  • Eliminate plastic packaging from our products by 2023

We are committed to sustainability not just in our operations, but in our products as well. We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, healthy, and sustainably grown food, and we're working to make that a reality.

To learn more about our sustainability initiatives and how you can get involved, please contact us.
